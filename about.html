About Us

Debora Lima

Debora, born in the city of Porto, Portugal, began her dance career at a very young age. She has developed skills in various dance styles such as kizomba, ballroom, hip hop, and contemporary, performing in different countries including Italy and Australia. During her time in Australia, she started training in acrobatics and Latin dances. In 2013, she relocated to London, United Kingdom, where she specialized in Kizomba and became the UK champion in 2016. In 2018, she embarked on her professional career as a bachata dancer, alongside her current dance partner Edi. Debora's extensive journey as a dancer has made her one of the most recognized international and national dancers. She is the creator and choreographer of dance companies Eclipse Dance and Sexi Mamis.

Edi Alva

Edi was born in Lima, Peru. In contrast to Debora, he discovered his passion for dance at the age of 28 while living in Madrid, Spain. He received training from internationally renowned instructors and masters in Ballet, Latin dances, and Hip Hop. In 2011, he won two amateur bachata championships. Edi also worked as a Latin dance teacher in various cities in Spain, eventually establishing his own dance school and organizing several events until 2018. He then relocated to London, United Kingdom, where he continued teaching Latin dances. It was during this time that he and Debora solidified their partnership as international and national dance performers. Together, they have achieved a recognized career in the world of Latin dance. Edi is also the co-creator and choreographer of Eclipse Dance.

Edi & Debora

Edi and Debora met in London in 2018, and in October of that same year, they officially became professional dance partners specializing in Latin rhythms. Through their combined and arduous work, they have gained recognition as dancers both nationally and internationally, showcasing their art wherever they go. They have participated in numerous significant events in the United Kingdom, Europe, and now in the United States. Their partnership is an explosive blend when it comes to preparing workshops and choreographies, benefiting both themselves and their dance companies. It is worth noting that they also incorporate professional acrobatics into their performances and even offer workshops on the subject in the UK. They are the runners-up of the Bachata Cup 2023 in France. If you haven't had the chance to meet or learn from them, don't hesitate to get in touch with them.
